The mysterious, ever-masked rapper—the one whose “Fuck The Summer Up” Jay-Z famously built his first Tidal summer playlist around—is a proud showman. Giving fans a memorable experience is half of why she wears a mask in the first place. In her live performances, as in her music videos, she’s theatrical—a character who can be equal parts menacing (her masks give her the look of a bank robber) and sprightly. She’s small, but the colorfulness of her costumes and her persona makes her impossible to lose track of.
